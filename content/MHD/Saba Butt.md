Saba Butt is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist in the states of New York and New Jersey. Saba has worked with diverse client populations in community mental health settings and in-home behavioral support services. She has experience providing individual and family therapy with adults and adolescents. Saba has worked with clients experiencing depression, anxiety, interpersonal issues, and trauma. <br>
Her areas of interest include women’s mental health, religious/spiritual identity exploration, and the immigrant experience. She is also working towards her Perinatal Mental Health Certification (PMH-C), which allows her to assist mothers, fathers, birthing individuals, and families experiencing mood and anxiety disorders during pregnancy and up to a year postpartum, as well as those who have experienced birth trauma and/or perinatal loss. <br>
Saba aims to establish a safe and collaborative environment for clients where they can pursue self-exploration and employ their existing potential. <br>
