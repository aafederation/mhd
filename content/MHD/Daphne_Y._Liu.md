I’m a clinical psychologist specializing in relationship therapy, working with individuals and all parties in distressed loving relationships to strengthen connections, navigate challenges, and make meaningful decisions. My work is grounded in evidence-based approaches, including Integrative Behavioral Couple Therapy, the Gottman Method, and Emotionally Focused Therapy. I also provide evidence-supported treatments such as cognitive behavioral therapies for other mental health concerns (e.g., depression, anxiety) to individuals and all parties in relationships who struggle with these mental health challenges. I take a compassionate, collaborative, and strengths-based approach. Together, we’ll clarify what matters most to you and your relationships. I bring clinical expertise while honoring you as the expert of your lived experience. I welcome clients of all identities and am LGBTQIA+-affirming and polyamorous-friendly, with a multicultural, identity-aware lens.
