As a NY State licensed clinical social worker (LCSW), I bring specialized training in evidence-based therapies to my work with individuals and groups. This includes, Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP) for obsessive-compulsive disorder and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) for chronic worry, health anxiety, performance and test anxiety, panic, phobias, misophonia, insomnia, trauma, and body-focused repetitive behaviors (BFRBs). I also integrate mindfulness-based approaches, including Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), to address social anxiety, perfectionism, chronic illness/pain, burnout, and self-acceptance or identity concerns. <br>
I hold a Master’s in Social Work from Columbia University, where I specialized in clinical practice with a focus on bodily health, mental health, and disability, and a B.A. in Psychology from Emory University <br>
