Ann Lee completed her Masters degree in Counseling at the Alliance Graduate School of Counseling. She is a Licensed Professional Counselor in the State of New Jersey (License #37PC00724100) and a Licensed Mental Health Counselor in the State of New York (License #017441). She also holds a BS in Economics from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and has worked in corporate finance for several years before making a career change to counseling. She is passionate about helping restore people from a place of brokenness to a place of healing. Her areas of focus include depression, anxiety, grief and loss, trauma, marital/relationship difficulties, women’s issues, career concerns, parenting and overall lifestyle adjustments.  Additionally, she is an EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) therapist.  Ann has a heart for the Asian-American community, having grown up in this sub-culture herself. However, her clinical experience has included people of all backgrounds and walks of life.
