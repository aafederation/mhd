I am a Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker (LICSW) based in Washington, DC, providing psychotherapy to adults and couples both in person and virtually in the states where I’m licensed. My work focuses on grief, trauma, anxiety, depression, and life transitions, with particular sensitivity to the layered experiences of cultural identity, high achievement, family expectations, and intergenerational dynamics. <br>
As an Indian American clinician with a background in global mental health and social work, I bring both clinical expertise and lived cultural understanding into the therapy room. I specialize in EMDR therapy for trauma, integrate CBT for practical skill-building, and incorporate IFS-informed parts work to help clients develop greater self-compassion and internal clarity. In couples therapy, I draw from the Gottman Method and Emotionally Focused Therapy to support deeper connection and more secure attachment. <br>
My style is warm, thoughtful, and direct. I aim to create a space where clients feel deeply understood while also gently challenged toward growth. Therapy with me is collaborative, culturally attuned, and grounded in evidence-based practice. <br>
