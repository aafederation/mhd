Mandy Lam (she/her) is a licensed clinical social worker, licensed creative arts therapist, and board-certified music therapist practicing virtually with adults in New York and New Jersey. Her work is grounded in EMDR, IFS-informed approaches, and somatic methods, with a focus on the mind-body connection and what gets stored in the body when words alone aren't enough. She weaves music therapy into sessions in ways that go beyond traditional talk therapy. Mandy specializes in high-functioning anxiety, people-pleasing and over-functioning, AAPI and immigrant identity, perinatal mental health, and relationship patterns. She works with adults who are self-aware and high-achieving but feel stuck, disconnected from themselves, or worn down by carrying too much for too long. She offers sessions in English, Cantonese, and Mandarin.
