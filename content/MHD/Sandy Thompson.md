Trained at Columbia University with over a decade of experience, Dr. Thompson specializes in cognitive behavioral therapy for anxiety, people-pleasing, self-esteem, and relationship and boundary challenges. Her style is warm, collaborative and direct. Whether you’re dealing with stress, self-doubt, or feeling stuck in life, she'll help you understand your experiences with self-compassion, and create observable shifts that help life feel more balanced and fulfilling. <br>
Dr. Thompson will tailor your work together to help you gain clarity and navigate challenges in your career, relationships, or family life in a way that feels grounded and effective. In a supportive, non-judgmental space, you’ll explore patterns that may be holding you back, build on your strengths, and practice new ways of engaging with life that align with your values and goals. <br>
Starting therapy can feel daunting, but change is possible. Dr. Thompson will support you every step of the way. You'll go at a pace that feels right for you, so you feel empowered to understand yourself more deeply and build a life that reflects what matters most to you. If you're looking for a proactive, compassionate therapist, she'd love to take the first step with you! <br>
Dr. Thompson is fluent in English and Mandarin and has both in-person and remote therapy availability.
