Jonathan Oca is a Licensed Master Social Worker providing individual therapy for adults navigating substance use recovery, trauma, anxiety, depression, life transitions, and neurodivergence. <br>
With experience in outpatient addiction treatment, crisis intervention, and community mental health, Jonathan integrates Brainspotting (Phases 1 & 2), motivational interviewing, mindfulness, CBT, and a strengths-based approach to support emotional regulation, trauma processing, and personalized coping strategies. <br>
His therapy style is calm, compassionate, non-judgmental, and trauma-informed—creating a safe space for curiosity, pause, and genuine healing. <br>
Jonathan holds an MSW from Stony Brook University and works under the clinical supervision of Meghan McLeod, LCSW, at Mosaic Mind Psychotherapy. He is currently accepting new clients. <br>
